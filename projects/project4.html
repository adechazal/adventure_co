Ski Touring Intermediate: 01. – 04.03

Around Bivio and Jenatsch Area (4 days)

Overview

We start our ski touring trip based from a hotel in Bivio. Then we move to the glamorous St. Moritz and discover a remote area around the Jenatsch hut. All together, a very interesting time in the Swiss Alps awaits!

Day 1: Arrival and Initial Tour

  • Meeting Point: Meet at 10:00 AM at the Hotel Post in Bivio (1769m).
  • Guide Pickup: On the drive to Bivio, the group will pick up our mountain guide in Landquart.
  • Hotel Check-In & Local Exploration: After check-in at Hotel Post, walk to the nearby small ski area and access the slopes via T-bar lifts.
  • First Ski Tour: Begin your first ski tour in the area, with various options including Roccabella (2731m), Piz Scalotta (2991m), Uf da Flüe (2774m), Piz Lunghin (2780m), Piz Turba (3017m) and other rewarding routes.

Day 2 & Day 3: Diverse Daily Ski Tours

Every day, you will have the opportunity to do diversified ski tours in this fantastic area. Choose from a variety of summits and rewarding routes that suit your pace and interests.

Day 4: Sunday, March 3rd – St. Moritz & Jenatsch Hut

  • Departure to Julier Pass: Drive to the Julier Pass parking spot and park your car.
  • Transfer to St. Moritz: An organized taxi will take the group to St. Moritz.
  • Cable Car Ride: Use the cable car to ascend to Piz Nair (3022m).
  • Skiing Down: Ski down a piste to the Suvretta Pass (2615m).
  • Skinning Up: Skin up to Fuorcla Suvretta (2966m) in approximately 1½ hours.
  • Descent & Final Ascent: After enjoying the view, ski down into Val Bever until approximately 2400m, then complete the day with an uphill to Chamanna Jenatsch (2652m) – the highest mountain hut in Graubünden (approx. 1 hour).

Day 5: March 4th – Final Ski Tour & Return

  • Morning Tour: After a hearty breakfast, set out on the final tour of the trip.
  • Route Options: Choose between ascending to the peak of Piz Surgonda (3193m) for approximately 2½ hours, or heading to Fuorcla d’Agnel (2982m) for about 1½ hours.
  • Descent: Enjoy the final descent back to the Julier Pass parking spot.
