Epic Davos Ski

Embark on a multi-day journey through pristine alpine landscapes with our expertly guided ski tour. Whether you’re looking for a thrilling descent down untouched powder or a serene day exploring remote mountain huts, this itinerary blends exhilarating skiing, cultural immersion, and gourmet dining to create an unforgettable adventure.

Day 1: Arrival, Preparation & Alpine Exploration

  • Arrival & Transfers: Land and enjoy seamless airport transfers to a welcoming alpine base hotel, where you’re greeted with a warm welcome and local drinks.
  • Equipment Rental: After settling in, head out early to pick up your rental car and high-quality ski equipment from our trusted partner, ensuring a perfect fit.
  • Groomed Slope Run: If conditions are ideal, take a brief, invigorating run on nearby groomed slopes to get your adrenaline pumping.
  • Mountain Resort Visit: In the afternoon, travel to a renowned mountain resort where alpine charm meets modern convenience and cap off the day with a hearty dinner at a traditional restaurant.

Day 2: Ski Resort Experience & Pre-Touring Fun

  • Resort Day: Start your day with a lift-assisted ski session at a popular resort (tickets pre-booked for your convenience). Soak in the panoramic views while carving fresh tracks.
  • Mountain Lunch: Savor a scenic lunch at a peak-side restaurant that offers both comfort food and breathtaking vistas.
  • Afternoon Relaxation: Return to your base to review the upcoming touring routes and enjoy some downtime.
  • Evening Social: Join your fellow adventurers for post-ski drinks and a themed dinner at a hand-picked local venue.

Day 3: Guided High-Alpine Ski Touring

  • Expert Guidance: Meet our experienced mountain guide who will lead you on a challenging yet rewarding ski tour.
  • Ascent to the Peaks: Begin with an ascent—via cable car or a scenic drive—to the starting point of your tour. Strap on your skins and set off toward a breathtaking summit, where the panoramic alpine views are truly awe-inspiring.
  • Mountain Lunch: Recharge with a lunch served in a picturesque on-mountain setting.
  • Descent & Evening: After an invigorating descent back to the base, celebrate the day’s triumph with a group dinner at a top-rated local steakhouse or dining establishment.

Day 4: Extended Tour & Remote Mountain Experience

  • Venture Off the Beaten Path: Enjoy another guided expedition that takes you deeper into remote alpine terrain. Choose from several scenic routes that offer summits with spectacular vistas and diverse terrain challenges.
  • Alpine Hospitality: Stop for a hearty mountain lunch and, if the spirit moves you, visit a traditional mountain hut to experience authentic alpine hospitality.
  • Casual Evenings: Wind down with a relaxed dinner at a local favorite, sharing stories of the day’s exploits with new friends.

Day 6: Final Tour & Departure

  • Final Tour: Kick off the day with one last exhilarating ski touring adventure to explore hidden peaks and memorable routes.
  • Mountain Lunch: Enjoy a hearty mountain lunch while soaking in the alpine scenery.
  • Scenic Descent: Embark on a scenic descent back to base.
  • Farewell Dinner & Departure: Conclude with a festive farewell dinner at a local restaurant, followed by direct transfers to the airport.

Tour Highlights & Practical Information

  • Tailored for Intermediate Skiers: This tour is perfect for skiers comfortable on moderate to steep terrain, familiar with switchback turns, and experienced with ski crampons.
  • Expert-Led Experience: Enjoy the assurance of an experienced mountain guide, who brings local insights and ensures safety on every ascent and descent.
  • Accommodation & Meals: Stay in a mix of cozy alpine hotels and traditional mountain huts, with half-board options that let you experience authentic local flavors.
  • Flexible & Adaptable: While our itinerary is carefully planned, we also offer the flexibility to adapt routes and experiences based on daily conditions and group preferences.

Discover the perfect blend of adrenaline, scenic beauty, and alpine culture with our Ultimate Alpine Ski Touring Adventure—a journey crafted for those who seek both challenge and charm in the heart of the mountains.
